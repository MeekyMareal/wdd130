About Us-Exciting Expeditions

Come and Enjoy the Adventure at George Rafting Company George Rafting Company offers exciting white-water rafting adventures. You can experience the fun with family or by yourself. With great equipment and professional guides, you are sure to have a great time during your adventure. Whether you are a thrill seeker or you want to relax and just float down the river, George Rafting Company can meet your needs. We offer great customer service and strive to provide you with a quality experience and a great adventure that you will want to repeat again and again.
