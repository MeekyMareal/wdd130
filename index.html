George Ekpo | WDD 130

My name is George Asukwo Ekpo. I am from Akwa Ibom State, Nigeria. Currently, I live in Benin City, studying. My interests include acquiring new knowledge, mastering new skills, especially technological skills. One of the things I have always been interested in is the internet, and how sites were created using it. I was fascinated by their content and overall appearance. That is why I started learning web development. So far, I have learned HTML and CSS. I plan to continue studying and gain enough expertise to develop my websites without any help from others in the future. One of the things I have learned while studying web development is that mistakes are necessary to understand certain concepts fully. In the beginning, it was quite upsetting when I could not manage to fix my code. However, with time, I have realized that mistakes are a part of the learning process. Every error teaches a person something new and helps them become a better programmer. I believe God is the one who grants me patience and wisdom, and gives me the ability to keep going despite failures. I hope He will continue to be with me on my path to becoming a great web developer. And I believe that with enough effort and faith, nothing is impossible. I have the determination and desire to learn web development. With God’s help and constant practice, I know I will succeed.
